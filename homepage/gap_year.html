My Gap Year


During the lockdown of 2020, I had to make a decision, and it was an important one.

Do I apply for college or do I take a gap year?


After a lot of browsing the web, asking people for their opinions and talking to myself, I finally decided. "I'm going to take a Gap Year"

So then I decided to make a list of things that I want to do in my gap year

  • Travel
  • Learn
  • Experience
  • Enjoy
